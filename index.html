About me

Hi, I am Ali Qureshi. I am a UX/UI designer with 7 years of experience in Graphic Design. I specialize in UX/UI Design, Graphic & Web Design and enjoy solving real-life problems. I approach my design-thinking with the double-diamond process; Phase 1: Research (Design the right thing), Phase 2: Design (Design things right)


My Toolbelt:

I enjoy creating, telling stories, working with other talented individuals that are like-minded. I have been told that I have a good eye for colour combinations, which I believe resonates through my portfolio. I am the black sheep of my family, daring to break out-of-the box and ask those difficult questions.
